Welcome to Dreamland Park

Welcome to Dreamland Park! If you’re looking for a place where you could hang out with your friends and family for an affordable price, then you’re looking at the perfect place.

We offer many fun rides to enjoy, a mini-golf course, and as well as an indoor arcade with a lunch/snack bar.
Our park also has scheduled shows that kids and parents can watch and enjoy. We also have birthday parties for parents who want to give their loved one something special to remember.

Birthday Parties
Have an upcoming birthday for your loved one(s)? Book a birthday party here! We will have a birthday room and will offer cake along with special celebration and the ticket blaster along with unlimited riders for the day.

Activities
We have a snack bar with a variety of foods. We will have pizza, fries, nuggets, tenders, salads, noodles, candy, chocolate , and many more.
We have a mini-golf course! We have 18 holes but divided into 2 sections of 9 holes. As you go further and further, you will explore many atmospheres from a variety of places!

Scheduled Shows ( Kids Theatre)

Tired from having too much fun or if you just want a break? We have special shows throughout the day where we create skits and scenes for the audience to watch us perform.
